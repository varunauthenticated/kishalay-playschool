SUMMER VACATION

Away from the scorching heat of summer sun, kids get the opportunity to refresh themselves during the school’s summer holidays. Both parents and children need some time to escape the soaring mercury levels. A family trip to a hill station or a cooler zone will be just the right choice. Or one can even look for development of skills, creative learning, and dance or sports classes to keep children indulged all the time.

Here we have some tips that could make their vacations a merry time:-

label_important

Summer games: children can involve in indoor and outdoor games. It will be wise to schedule outdoor games during the cooler hours of evening or early morning. Their clothes need to be loose, light colored and of cotton. Always supervise them when they are in the playground.

label_important

Creative zone: television and smart phone hours should be lessened. Storytelling sessions, educational games and walk in the nature should be introduced in their schedule. Hobbies should be encouraged more.

label_important

Diet: summer heat can dehydrate children more often than adults, so care should be taken that they consume optimum fluid. Fruits should be a part of their meals. Lessen the quantity of oily food and junks. You can always serve them homemade popsicles and cakes which would be a yummy and soothing treat to both parent and child.
