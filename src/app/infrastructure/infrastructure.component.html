Activity Arena
Activity Arena

Our activity section provides children the amenities to experiment with objects, learn about their shapes and colors, handle art stuffs to create crafts, share space with others and learn. We have in stock: interactive play boards, abacus, join-the-beads toys, colorful alphabet mats and lots more to make learning so much fun.

Audio-Visual Room
Audio-Visual Room

AV rooms are a boot to their preschool learning. Here our toddlers can enjoy watching animated nursery rhymes and refresh themselves. ‘A great way of learning is through visuals’.

Outdoor Activities
Outdoor Activities

Outdoor rollick, spending time with nature and strolling round the garden under the open sky are all indelible memories of one’s childhood. We let your children enjoy a day out in the sun and make friends with bunnies and birds; all important for their holistic development.

Classroom
Classroom

Toddlers here learn alphabets, numbers, rhymes, body parts, everyday basic science and lots more through story telling sessions and listen-and-say along techniques. Our teachers help them build their vocabulary, listening and speaking skills through a multitude of creative learning experiences.
