About Us

Our Philosophy

Kishalay recognizes need and pace of the individual child. We aim to make each child comfortable through play and fun.

Children learn better in a relaxed atmosphere by observing nature, playing and chatting with each other and singing songs and reciting nursery rhymes.

Through carefully planned activities we encourage children to improve their vocabulary, listen, talk and respond as well as to develop their writing skills at a very tender age.

With open hearts we welcome all seasons and festivities that celebrate them.

We provide opportunities for kids to be active and interactive. At the same time we also help them understand the importance of physical activities, yoga and a healthy diet.

Children learn about shapes, colours and sizes through everyday activities. Our creative platform enhances the same ability by keeping a day for each vivid colour or shape.
